Hindu pilgrimage sites in India

Kedarnath

The Kedarnath temple in Uttarakhand is a sacred Hindu pilgrimage site and one of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva. It is also part of the Chota Char Dham pilgrimage circuit.

Ujjain

Ujjain was once one of the primary cities in Middle India, and served as the cultural, political, and literary centre around 600 BC. The city has been witness to the rise and fall of numerous empires, and also finds mention in the literary works of stalwarts including Kalidas.
